# VerityAir Systems Ltd Investor Pitch

## Naming

- Company: VerityAir Systems Ltd
- Platform: VerityATLAS

## 30-Second Pitch

VerityAir Systems Ltd is building VerityATLAS, a mission assurance platform for regulated drone operations. VerityATLAS helps operators plan, assess, execute, monitor, and evidence missions through one assurance-led workflow, connecting mission risk, airspace compliance, pilot and platform readiness, live operational context, replay, audit evidence, and now operator-governance controls around Operational Authorisation, insurance, and assigned personnel. As drone operations move toward more complex and BVLOS environments, the operational gap is no longer just flight planning, it is proving that the mission was safe, authorised, and accountable end to end.

## 2-Minute Pitch

Commercial drone operations are growing, but safe and scalable operations in regulated airspace still break down across disconnected tools, manual checks, and weak audit trails. Operators can plan a mission, but proving readiness, tracking live operational context, and showing who approved what and why is still messy and inconsistent.

VerityATLAS addresses that problem as an assurance-led FSMS platform for UAS operations. In the current build, it already covers mission planning, mission lifecycle state, mission risk assessment, airspace compliance inputs, pilot readiness, platform readiness, telemetry replay, external overlays for weather and traffic context, conflict assessment, audit evidence snapshots, and post-operation evidence flows.

That matters because the operational bottleneck in this market is not just navigation. It is assurance continuity from planning through live operations to post-mission evidence, all inside the operator's real compliance envelope. Our aim is to become the operational assurance layer serious drone operators rely on when they need missions to be safe, explainable, and defensible.

The founder brings deep aerospace operational credibility from military and civilian aviation environments, which gives VerityAir Systems Ltd unusually strong founder-market fit for a software product in this category.

The commercial delivery model is cloud-first. VerityATLAS is intended to operate as a multi-company assurance platform delivered through SaaS, and the build now includes the first real tenant-isolation and authenticated membership controls across sensitive document and governance workflows. Higher-isolation deployment options remain the later path for enterprise and defence customers who need single-tenant or private environments.

The next step is not inventing the product category. It is proving operator dependence through design partners, pilot deployments, and measurable reductions in planning friction, readiness gaps, and audit burden.
