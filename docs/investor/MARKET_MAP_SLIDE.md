# VerityATLAS Market Map Slide

## Naming

- Company: VerityAir Systems Ltd
- Platform: VerityATLAS

## Slide Title

Market Map: Mission Assurance For Regulated Drone Operations

## Slide Copy

### Direct Competitors

- **Aloft**: airspace, authorizations, fleet, flight ops
- **FlyFreely**: mission planning, compliance, fleet workflow
- **DroneDeploy**: enterprise ops, pilot and equipment workflow
- **Airspace Link**: airspace, risk, compliance, U.S. approvals
- **AirData**: flight data, compliance, maintenance, reporting

### Adjacent Competitors

- **Unifly**: UTM, approvals, authority workflows
- **SkyGrid**: airspace, ops, weather, integration
- **OneSky**: UTM and operations-center support
- **Droniq**: U-Space, conformance, BVLOS planning

### Historical UK Benchmark

- **Altitude Angel**: important UK benchmark, not a current active competitor

### Why VerityATLAS Wins

- **One assurance chain**: planning to audit in one record
- **Accountable by design**: who decided what, when, why
- **Built for regulated operators**: OA, readiness, evidence, oversight
- **Aviation logic, not just drone convenience**

## Presenter Notes

- Do not say VerityATLAS has no competitors. That sounds naive.
- Do say most competitors solve slices of the workflow.
- Keep the contrast simple:
  - direct competitors help manage drone operations
  - adjacent competitors help manage airspace and future traffic integration
  - VerityATLAS is building the assurance and evidence layer across the mission lifecycle
- If Altitude Angel comes up, treat it as evidence that the market need is real but the business model and timing of UTM infrastructure players can be fragile.
- If challenged on UTM players, position VerityATLAS as complementary rather than claiming to replace national or authority-grade traffic systems.
